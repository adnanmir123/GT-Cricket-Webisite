About Us

The Georgia Tech Cricket Club was established in 2010 with the mission to promote cricket on campus and provide students with opportunities to play competitive cricket.

Our club competes in the Southeastern Collegiate Cricket Conference and has won multiple championships over the years. We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced cricketers.

The club provides equipment, coaching, and regular practice sessions for all members. We also organize friendly matches with other universities and local cricket clubs.

Practice Schedule

Regular practice sessions are held every Tuesday and Thursday from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM at the Campus Recreation Center fields.

Additional optional practice sessions are available on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
